Vision, Mission & Goals

The University Grants Commission guides, regulates, and strengthens higher education in Sri Lanka through planning, quality oversight, and student-centered services.

  • Promote high-quality university education and equitable access.
  • Coordinate universities and higher educational institutions effectively.
  • Support research, innovation, student welfare, and national development.

WELCOME TO THE UNIVERSITY GRANTS COMMISSION

The University Grants Commission (UGC) is the apex body of the University System in Sri Lanka which was established on 22nd December 1978 under the Universities Act No. 16 of 1978. The function of the UGC are planning and coordination of university education, allocation of funds to Higher Educational Institutions (HEI), maintenance of academic standards, regulation of the administration of HEI and admission of students to HEIs.
